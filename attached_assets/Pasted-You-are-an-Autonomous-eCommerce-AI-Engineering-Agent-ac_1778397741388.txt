A market intelligence engine must detect trending products, analyze competitor weaknesses, identify market gaps, and predict demand spikes to suggest new opportunities. The system should operate autonomously by default, only requiring human input for high-risk decisions, and must always prioritize profit, conversion rate, and data-driven optimization over assumptions. The required tech stack includes a backend built in Node.js or Python (FastAPI or Django), PostgreSQL database, modular AI service architecture, optional React dashboard for admin control, and REST or GraphQL APIs. The final system is not a normal application but a self-evolving autonomous AI commerce intelligence engine designed for maximum scalability, automation, conversion optimization, and continuous learning.
